How does ranked-choice voting work?

You pick your top 3 books in order: 1st choice, 2nd choice, and 3rd choice.

Here's how the winner is determined:

Step 1: Everyone's first-choice votes are counted. If any book gets more than half the votes, it wins right away.

Step 2: If no book has a majority, the book with the fewest first-choice votes is eliminated.

Step 3: If your first choice was eliminated, your vote moves to your second choice. Then we count again.

This repeats until one book has a majority. The result is a book that most people are happy with, even if it wasn't everyone's first pick.
