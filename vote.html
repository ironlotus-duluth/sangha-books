How does ranked-choice voting work?

Instead of picking just one book, you rank all the books from your most-preferred (1st) to least-preferred (last).

Here's how the winner is determined:

Step 1: Everyone's first-choice votes are counted. If any book gets more than half the votes, it wins right away.

Step 2: If no book has a majority, the book with the fewest first-choice votes is eliminated.

Step 3: If your first choice was eliminated, your vote transfers to your next choice. Then we count again.

This repeats until one book has a majority. The result is that the winning book is one that most people are happy with, even if it wasn't everyone's first pick.
