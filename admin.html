How to run a round (step by step)

Each round follows a simple lifecycle. Here's what to do at each stage:

1. Create a new round

Use the form below to pick a name (e.g. "Fall 2026") and a password. The password is what you'll share with the group so they can access the site. Submissions open automatically when you create a round.

2. Collect book suggestions

Send the group the link (books.ironlot.us) and the round password. They'll enter the password and can start suggesting books. You can see all submissions on the "Book Submissions" page, or directly in the Google Sheet under the "Suggestions" tab. To remove a mistaken entry, just delete the row in the sheet.

3. Close submissions, open voting

Once you have enough book suggestions, come back here and click "Close Submissions" on the active round. Then click "Open Voting". Let the group know it's time to vote. They'll rank the books by dragging them into their preferred order.

4. Wait for votes

Give the group a week or so to vote. You can check progress on the "Vote Results" page anytime — results update live. If someone provides their email when voting, they'll get an automatic confirmation.

5. Complete the round

When everyone has voted (or you're ready to call it), click "Complete Round". This closes voting and moves the round into the "Past Rounds" archive, which anyone can view without a password.
